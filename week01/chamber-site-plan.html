[Location] Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is [Location] Chamber of Commerce. This name reflects the city or region served and emphasizes the primary mission of promoting local businesses and economic growth.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is:

  • To provide a platform for businesses to connect, collaborate, and grow.
  • To inform the public about events, resources, and opportunities within the region.

Scenarios

The website will answer key questions for its target audience, such as:

  • What networking events are happening this month?
  • How can I join the chamber or learn about its benefits?
  • Where can I find resources for starting a business in the area?

Color Schema

The selected color palette for the site includes:

  • Primary Color: Blue (#0033A0) - Used for headings, links, and branding elements.
  • Accent Color: Gold (#FFD700) - Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

The selected fonts for the site include:

  • Heading Font: Roboto Condensed Regular 400 - Used for all headings.
  • Body Font: Roboto Condensed Light 300 - Used for body text and general content.
